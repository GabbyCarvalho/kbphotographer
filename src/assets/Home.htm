Intentional Gatherings. Effortless Style

Hi, I’m Phoenix —
A mama, creative, and experience-maker obsessed with turning everyday moments into extraordinary memories. I believe the best gatherings aren’t just pretty—they’re personal, thoughtful, and designed to make everyone feel welcome.

Brightfolk Gatherings grew out of a season of rediscovery, when I found my way back to joy, artistry, and connection beyond motherhood. Before curating picnics, I spent years in live event production, creating VIP experiences for concerts, festivals, and tours. That background taught me the art of detail, hospitality, and unforgettable first impressions—skills I now pour into every gathering I style.

Whether you’re planning a proposal, a brand shoot, a bridal brunch, or a slow afternoon with your favorite people, I’ll bring the setup, styling, and ambiance that makes your day effortless and unforgettable. You just show up, relax, and enjoy—because life’s best moments deserve to be savored.
